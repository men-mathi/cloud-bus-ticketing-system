About Smart Bus Connect

Smart Bus Connect is a cloud-based public transport ticketing system that simplifies bus booking across cities. Passengers can search buses, select seats visually, make secure payments and receive QR based tickets instantly.

Our Mission

Our goal is to modernize traditional bus ticketing systems using cloud technology. TransitCloud provides a fast, secure and user-friendly platform for passengers and transport operators. By integrating digital ticketing, seat management and QR verification, we ensure a seamless travel experience.

Key Features

Online Bus Booking

Passengers can easily search and book buses between cities with just a few clicks.

Seat Selection

Interactive seat layout allows passengers to visually choose available seats.

Secure Payments

Users can complete transactions safely through a digital payment system.

QR Code Ticket

Instant QR code tickets reduce paper usage and allow quick verification.
